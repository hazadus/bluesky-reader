💬 Python Tip #77 (of 365):

Need to check for the presence of prefixes or suffixes? Use startswith or endswith. 🧵

Instead of this:
test_key = (API_KEY[:5] == "test-")

Do this:
test_key = API_KEY.startswith("test-")
