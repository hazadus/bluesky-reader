💬 Python Tip #74 (of 365):

Print blank lines with print()

Instead of this:
print("TITLE\n")
print("Descrption")

Consider this:
print("TITLE")
print()
print("Descrption")

It's easier to see there's meant to be a blank line when a separate print call is used.
