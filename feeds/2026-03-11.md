💬 Python Tip #70 (of 365):

Print partial lines by specifying end="" 🧵

Instead of writing to "sys.stdout" to avoid printing newlines:

sys.stdout.write(prefix)
sys.stdout.write(remainder + "\n")

Consider passing end to print():

print(prefix, end="")
print(remainder)
