💬 Python Tip #100 (of 365):

Prefer the pathlib.Path object's is_file() and is_dir() methods over exists()

Using is_file() or is_dir() is more explicit than exists().

Usually when I find myself using exists(), I realize that I wanted one of the more specific methods instead.
